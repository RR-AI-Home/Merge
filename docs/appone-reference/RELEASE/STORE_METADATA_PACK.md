## Full Description
Cricket Rivals is a mobile-first cricket management game built around fair-play progression, club identity, and clear tactical choices.

Build your squad, lock your Playing XI, set your strategy, scout opponents, and take your club through:
- Challenge ladder progression
- Knockout Cup runs
- Tower PvE climbs
- Training, injuries, morale, and youth decisions
- Team Stats and player-detail tracking

The game is designed around a zero-power economy:
- no pay-to-win shortcuts
- no stat boosts for money
- no unfair competitive advantages

Planned monetization focuses on cosmetics, themes, presentation, and optional rewarded moments without changing match fairness.
